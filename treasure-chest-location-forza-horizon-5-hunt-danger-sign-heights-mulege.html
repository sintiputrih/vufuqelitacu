Treasure Chest Location - Forza Horizon 5 Treasure Hunt Danger Sign Heights of Mulege

The Forza Horizon 5 treasure chest location in the Heights of Mulege Danger Sign treasure hunt is in the general area of southeast Baja California, but actually getting to it is way more complicated than just that. You first have to run through the danger sign and get a good amount of air to get the treasure clue. Then, you have to search through the marked area to actually find the chest. It’s all a bit complicated, really. That’s why we’ve made this guide to help you out.

Forza Horizon 5 Treasure Chest Location – Treasure Hunt Danger Sign Heights of Mulege

To find the treasure chest clue location in the Forza Horizon 5 Danger Sign Heights of Muelege treasure hunt, you first have to complete the jump.
