Tarantinos Inglourious Farmhouse Revealed!

Under normal circumstances, a photograph of a farmhouse would be, at best, pretty and quaint and, at worst, totally lame and boring. Leave it to Quentin Tarantino to change all that. Images of a French farmhouse constructed for his hotly anticipated (and potentially wonderfully insane) film Inglorious Bastards have leaked to the Internet, and fans who have already read a draft of the script can barely contain their enthusiasm for a peek at the location of the film’s opening showdown between its female lead and the “Jew Hunter.” Also of note to hard-core Tarantino fanboys: In addition to the farmhouse shots, there is some photographic evidence to suggest that the movie may actually be released with the deliberately misspelled moniker Inglourious Basterds.
