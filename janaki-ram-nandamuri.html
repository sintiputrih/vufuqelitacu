Janaki Ram’s Father

One of the best character actor roles was in the movie “Lahiri Lahiri Lahirilo” which was released in 2002. From 1996-1999, he served as a member of the Andhra Pradesh Legislative Assembly. He also became a Cabinet Minister for Road Transport in 1996. Moreover, he became a candidate for the ‘Telugu Desam Party. He was elected to ‘Rajya Sabha’ in 2008.
