Anastasia Shubskaya- Bio, Age, Height, Single, Net Worth, Facts

Table of Biography

Anastasia Shubskaya is a famous professional model and film producer in Russia. Anastasia Shubskaya is famous as the wife of Russian ice hockey winger Alexander Mikhailovich Ovechkin, who captains the Washington Capitals of the National Hockey League (NHL).

Early Life and Education

Anastasia Shubskaya was born in Switzerland on November 16, 1993. She is currently 29 years old. She holds Russian nationality and her birth sign is Scorpio.

Her father’s name is Kirill Shubskiy and is a Russian businessman with an interest in the shipbuilding business her mother is Vera Glagolev.

Likewise, her mother was a Russian actress and film director popular for Russian films like ‘Poor Sasha,’ ‘Do Not Shoot at White Swans,’ and ‘At the End of the World.’

Also, she has two siblings named Anna and Maria who are from her mother’s first marriage with Soviet-American-Russian actor Rodion Nakhapetov.

She grew up in Switzerland along with her step-siblings, but her family later moved to Moscow, Russia. Her mother passed away because of cancer on August 16, 2017, at a hospital in Germany. She completed her high school studies in Moscow.

Moreover, she attended the Gerasimov Institute of Cinematography and completed her graduation in 2014. She also attended the New York Film Academy and did a 4-week acting program.

Anastasia Shubskaya – Relationship status

Anastasia was in a romantic relationship with businessman Artem Bolshakov for around three years. The couple even got engaged but rare meetings of the two due to Artem’s long stay in the US gradually affected their relationship leading to an eventual split.

Similarly, she attended the 2008 Summer Olympic Games held in Beijing, China, with her father. There she met the famous Russian professional ice hockey winger Alexander Mikhailovich Ovechkin for the first time.

In one of the after-parties, the young teenager got the chance to slow-dance with Alexander. Then in November 2014, Alexander found a picture of her on Instagram.

Eager to get in touch with her, Alexander managed to get her phone number and contacted her. As time passed, the two developed a strong friendship, which eventually blossomed into a romantic association.

Moreover, in September 2015, Alexander asked her parents for their consent and proposed to her with an engagement ring which she accepted. On September 11, he announced his engagement to her on Instagram.

On August 11, 2016, the couple married at the Fourth Wedding Palace at Butyrskaya in Moscow and now live in the US. The duo welcomed their son, on August 18, 2018. They named their baby boy Sergei Aleksandrovich Ovechkin after the late brother of Alexander.
