Eric Lloyd Bio (Wiki)

Who is Eric Lloyd?

Eric Lloyd is an American actor, producer, comedian, plus musician.

He started acting at a very small age. His known screen projects are The Wonder Years and The Santa Clause film trilogy.

Eric Lloyd- Birth Age, Family, Ethnicity

Eric Lloyd was born David Eric Lloyd Morelli in Glendale, California, U.S., on May 19, 1986.

His father is David Morelli and his mother is Melissa Lloyd Rogers. He also has a younger brother Emily Ann Lloyd.

His ethnicity is a mix of Italian, English, and Scottish.

In which school did Eric Lloyd attend?

He graduated from Chapman University, Orange, California with a BFA in film production.

Eric Lloyd- Career, Professional Life

Eric Lloyd’s screen career started at the age of two only. He appeared in an episode of the TV series The Wonder Years as Kevin Arnold.

It was comedy-drama series that aired on the ABC network from 1988 to 1993.

Movie Career

Eric’s 1993 appeared in a short film, Sunny’s Deliverance in the role of David. Then he was featured in movies Heart and Souls (1993), Greedy (1994), The Santa Claus (1994), Dunston Checks In (1996), Natman & Robin (1997), and more.

His recent appeared movies are The Santa Clause 2 (2002), The Santa Clause 3: The Escape Clause (2006), and ChromeSkull: Laid to Rest 2 (2011).

TV Series

The list of Eric’s TV projects are,

  • Laurie Hill (1992)
  • The Oz Kids (1996-1997)
  • Chameleon (1998)
  • Jesse (1998-2000)
  • ER (2001)
  • True Perfection (2012)

Musician and Producer

In 2018 and 2019, Eric Lloyd released and produced two albums, Shawn Penn: Robbin’ Right? and Sunny Gummy Hands respectively.

His audiobook The Fifth Vital Sign (2019) was released.

Eric Lloyd co-owns LP Studios which assists other filmmakers, bands, and YouTube artists.

He also sings and plays guitar in a band called Radiomason.
