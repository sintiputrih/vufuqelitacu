Basketball star LiAngelo Ball and Love &amp; Hip Hop celebrity Nikki Mudarris are expecting their first


LiAngelo Ball, aged 24, and Love & Hip Hop: Hollywood star Nikki Mudarris, aged 32, are about to become parents

“We’re both filled with excitement about becoming parents. We discussed having a life and kids when we first met, our goals, dreams, the life we wanted, and then it really happened,” the couple tells PEOPLE.

“We’re eagerly anticipating meeting our baby and starting this new chapter together,” they added. “It’s extremely exciting for both of us to be first-time parents and to experience everything together.”

Both Mudarris and Ball expressed that their family and friends were “so excited” to hear the news.

“The news was extremely thrilling for everyone in my family. However, everyone was quite shocked, and I think they’re still in disbelief even though my belly is huge!” says Mudarris. “Overall, everyone cannot wait for the baby to arrive. They’re overjoyed for me; I found love with an amazing man, and God blessed me with my baby – everything I really wanted is truly happening.”

Ball, who plays for the Greensboro Swarm of the NBA G League, mentioned that he was in Charlotte, North Carolina when he first learned the news and wanted to wait to tell others in person when he returned home to California.

“When I told my mom and dad, they were a bit shocked, but I think they were more excited than anything,” he said. “My mom was yelling and cheering in excitement. When I told my brothers, NBA stars LaMelo and Lonzo Ball, they were happy for me and also excited to become new uncles to my seed.”

The couple revealed that they know the sex of their baby on the way but will be keeping it private at this time. As for names, “ironically we picked our kid(s) names three months before I got pregnant, so when we found out, it was pretty easy to decide what we were going to name the baby.”
