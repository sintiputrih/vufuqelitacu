How Much Is Justine Lupe Net Worth? Is She Married Or Still Dating?

ABC’s Home Economics was all about three adult siblings navigating their life at very different levels of wealth. And Justine Lupe came into the play as Emily — ex-wife of Connor (one of the siblings).

But who is she in real life? Has she already married like her on-screen role? And what about her net worth? Get answers as you scroll down.

How Much Is Justine Lupe Net Worth?

Justine Lupe’s net worth was under $1.5 million as of 2021.

This actress on Home Economics is the one audiences likely recognize from her prior roles in movies and TV shows such as Mr. Mercedes (2019), The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel (2018), and Succession (2018).

But if that doesn’t ring a bell, here are some more of her TV credits — Harry’s Law (2012), Cristela (2015), Snowfall (2017), Sneaky Pete (2018), Madam Secretary (2018), The Non-Essentials (2020), and Home Economics (2021).

Overall, Justine and Germain were cherishing their dating life to the fullest.
