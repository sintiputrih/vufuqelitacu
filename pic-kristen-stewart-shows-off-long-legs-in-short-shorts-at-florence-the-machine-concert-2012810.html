PIC: Kristen Stewart Shows Off Long Legs in Short Shorts at Florence + the Machine Concert

Kristen Stewart has a new concert buddy — her dad.

The 22-year-old Twilight actress stepped out in Hollywood Saturday to attend the Florence + the Machine show at the Hollywood Bowl.

After being caught cheating with director Rupert Sanders in mid-July, Stewart has since reconciled with Robert Pattinson, her 26-year-old boyfriend of three years. But the couple has yet to publicly reunite. Instead, Stewart recruited her father John to accompany her to the show.

In August, TMZ reported that Stewart's mother Jules filed for divorce from John after 27 years of marriage.
