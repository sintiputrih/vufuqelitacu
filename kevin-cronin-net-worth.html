Kevin Cronin’s Net Worth

Kevin Cronin, the lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist for REO Speedwagon, has a net worth of $25 million. He is responsible for writing many of the band’s biggest hits such as “Keep on Loving You” and “I Can’t Fight This Feeling”.

In 2008, Cronin appeared on the television show Don’t Forget the Lyrics! where he earned $350,000 before forgetting the words to a Donna Summer song. Cronin and his wife Lisa have a son named Paris as well as a daughter named Holly and two sons, Josh and Shane who were born in 2010.

Today, Kevin and REO Speedwagon continue to perform and he appears on an infomercial for the Ultimate Rock Ballads CD by TimeLife which features songs from the band’s rise to success in the 1980s.
