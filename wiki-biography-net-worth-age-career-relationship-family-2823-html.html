Busta Rhymes, whose real name is Trevor George Smith Jr., is an American rapper renowned for his incredible talent in the world of Hip-Hop music. His unique style of rhyming, with multiple syllables and rapid-fire delivery, has set him apart in the industry. Hailing from the East Coast, Busta Rhymes made his mark as a member of the iconic group, Leaders Of The New School. However, what truly stands out about him is his role as a father.

Family and Children

Busta Rhymes is not just a musical sensation; he’s also a loving father to six children, each a testament to different chapters of his life. These six kids come from three different women, Joanne Wood, Jill Miskelly, and Rhonda Randall, spanning the years from 1993 to 2006.
