Beyonc Wears Christopher Kane Lace Jacket and Mini Skirt

  • Beyoncé just celebrated a friend's birthday by posting some seriously chic photos on Instagram.
  • In the new pics, Beyoncé can be seen wearing a Christopher Kane lace jacket, featuring several cut-out panels, with a matching mini skirt.
  • The superstar completed the look with some fringed heels by Area and a pair of Roberi & Fraud sunglasses.

Beyoncé regularly provides us with style inspiration, and her latest Instagram pics are a masterclass in minimal dressing.

Wearing a Christopher Kane two-piece set, taken from the designer's Spring/Summer 2019 collection, Beyoncé looked totally flawless as she posed for the new snaps. She wore the lace-bonded cut-out jacket without a shirt underneath, and co-ordinated it with a matching mini-skirt.

The Destiny's Child star accessorized the look with a pair of Roberi & Fraud shades and jewelry by Messika Paris. Plus, her crystal-fringe heels by Area were basically to die for.

AREA Crystal Fringe Heels

Beyoncé's Christopher Kane look was seen on the runway back in 2018. While the singer's outfit is created in the same fabric and cut-out style, it appears that Beyoncé's-version has been customized to create a much-shorter mini skirt, and a revealing jacket silhouette.

Victor VIRGILE//Getty Images

A model walks the runway at the Christopher Kane Ready to Wear Spring/Summer 2019 show at London Fashion Week in September 2018.

The star of The Lion King put in an appearance at the opening of the Tyler Perry Studio just last week. Wearing a figure-hugging gold dress by Yousef Aljasmi, with laboratory-grown diamond drop earrings by Anabela Chan, the singer looked gorgeous in the sparkly, show-stopping look.
