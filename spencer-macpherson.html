Spencer Macpherson Wiki, Age, Height, Biography, Girlfriend &amp; Family

Spencer Macpherson (born June 12, 1997) is a Canadian Actor, Model, and Social Media Personality from Mississauga, Canada. He became famous after he acted on the long-running TV series “Degrassi: The Next Generation“. Furthermore, the 21-years-old Actor made his acting debut with this show in 2014 and played a role of Hunter Hollingsworth.

Later, Spencer also starred in the shows Degrassi TV (2015) and Degrassi: Next Class (2016). Moreover, the actor made his debut on the silver screen with the movie titled “Extracurricular” as Ian Gordon in 2018. Later, he also starred in the TV movie Killer High as Tom in 2018.

Macpherson is also popular for his work as King Charles IX of France in the “Reign” (CW period drama show). In addition, Spencer recently joined the cast of the “Northern Rescue” (Netflix drama series) in 2019.

He is playing one of the main roles in the show along with stars like William Baldwin, Kathleen Robertson, Amalia Williamson etc. The young actor Macpherson plays the role of Scout West. The first episode of the Northern Rescue aired on March 1, 2019.

Profile, Parents & Early Life

The actor was born on the 12th of June 1997 in Mississauga, Canada. Hence, Spencer Macpherson’s age is 21 years old, as of 2018. His father Bobby Macpherson is a businessman and the actors’ mother Cheryl Macpherson is a homemaker.

Furthermore, Spencer belongs to White Caucasian ethnicity. He also has two elder sisters named Meagan and Rebecca Macpherson. The actor completed his school education from Cawthra Park Secondary School and graduated in 2015.
