Belvidere, North Carolina is mourning the recent loss of one of its cherished residents, Rusty Stallings. The exact cause of Rusty’s passing has not been disclosed, leaving family and friends grappling with the enormity of this sorrowful event.

Born and raised in Perquimans County, Rusty Stallings was a proud graduate of Perquimans County High School and a steadfast local presence throughout his life. His dedication to hard work was exemplified through his roles at Nucor Steel and as the owner of R&R Farms, embodying the true grit and perseverance emblematic of North Carolina.

However, it was Rusty’s warmth and camaraderie that left an indelible mark on those fortunate enough to know him intimately.

Brandon emphasized the depth of their connection, forged through hundreds of hunts that solidified their bond over a shared love for the great outdoors. Their story, destined to unfold over decades more, was regrettably cut short by Rusty’s passing.
