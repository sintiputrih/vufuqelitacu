Lacey Evans Promises ol Fashion Woopin For Naomi Next Week On WWE SmackDown

Naomi deserves better, but Lacey Evans doesn’t seem to think so. The Lady Of WWE and The Glow are currently engaged in a rivalry that will likely see the two battling for a while.

Click here for our complete coverage of WWE Friday Night SmackDown from this week.

Lacey Evans fired back at the idea of Naomi deserving better following SmackDown this week. She wasn’t happy at all about how things turned out for her. Now she has an “ol fashion woopin” to deliver to Naomi next week.

Naomi deserves better?!!!

Attacking classy, sophisticated women from behind while they are most vulnerable?

The ONLY thing she deserves is ANOTHER good ol fashion woopin.

Naomi and Lacey Evans will square off in singles competition next week on SmackDown. We’ll have to see how Vince McMahon books this situation. R-Truth was quick to point out that Evans was the one who attacked Naomi first.

Many fans agree that Naomi deserves better. It will be interesting to see if a win next week for Naomi is a sign of bigger things to come, or simply another case of WWE’s 50/50 booking.
