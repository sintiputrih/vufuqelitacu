Pregnant Lacey Chaberts Nursery Is a Chic Pink Dream

So sweet! Pregnant Lacey Chabert’s little girl has a stunningly chic nursery awaiting her. The Mean Girls actress, 33, enlisted the help of designer Vanessa Antonelli to make her dreams come true.

Antonelli, who also recently designed Bachelor alum Ali Fedotowsky’s nursery (who could forget the wall accented with thousands of silk roses?!), created a modern yet traditional space with a pink and gray color scheme.

Floral wallpaper by Tempaper mixed with a nailhead moulding offer a sweet yet unique take on the baby-to-be’s room.

The soft blush and gray Bella Motte bedding and linens combined with a faux fur ottoman by Best Home Fashions also add warmth to the cozy space. The cozy feel is juxtaposed by sleek hardwood floors. 

The room also features Franklin & Ben furniture, floral mirrors by Land of Nod and a Delta glider.

Chabert, who wore a Rachel Pally gown in the photos, announced her pregnancy via Twitter last February, posting an adorable photo of her Chihuahua, Kitty, wearing a teeny shirt that read: “My mom is having a baby & all I get is this silly shirt.”
