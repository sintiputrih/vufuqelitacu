What Turns Me On In Men Actress Honey Dee Reveals

Fast-rising Nigerian actress, Oyindamola Sanni better known as Honey Dee has opened up on her love life and what kind of man she would love to date.

Speaking to Sun Newspaper, Honey Dee revealed that she is single at the moment and not ready to mingle although she outlined some requirement she would look out for in a man before agreeing to be his wife.

She said,

 “I have been heartbroken. It was a very pathetic experience, very painful.Now the first thing I look out for in a man is; must be God fearing; and then he must be caring, understanding, and financially stable with good looks. Good looks, eloquence and nice colognes turn me on in a man. All these get me emotional kind of. But for now I am not in any serious relationship. I’m also not really ready to mingle.”
