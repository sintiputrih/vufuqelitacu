Emily Ratajkowski Flaunts Her Engagement Ring In Topless Instagram Photo

Emily Ratajkowski took to Instagram to show off her best assets in a new topless photo—including her dazzling two-stone diamond engagement ring.

Emrata and her hubby Sebastian Bear-McClard tied the knot in a non-traditional ceremony back in February, but she debuted her unique bling on Instagram for the first time just last week.

Because of their speedy engagement and wedding, Emrata didn't get her ring until long after McClard proposed.

"He proposed to me at Minetta Tavern, and he didn't have a ring so I was like hmmm, no. But then he took the paper clip that the bill was paid with and made me a ring which I actually thought was really romantic," she told Jimmy Fallon on The Tonight Show.

The supermodel and her actor/producer beau tied the knot in February with a small ceremony at a courthouse in New York City just a few weeks after they started dating. Just like her non-traditional ring, Emrata ditched the classic white wedding dress, and wore a yellow Zara suit and floppy hat for her ceremony.

"I never loved the idea of wearing white for a wedding because I'm not a pure woman. I'm a woman with a lot of character and experience and life. I also just loved the idea of a suit, especially at City Hall because it's like you're taking care of business," she told Vanity Fair.
