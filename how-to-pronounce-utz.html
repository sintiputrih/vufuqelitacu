How to pronounce utz

How do you pronounce Utz brand?

Utz is pronounced “Uhtz” as in “uhts”.

Does the Utz girl have a name?

It was the earliest design of the Little Utz Girl, an icon who stood as the embodiment of all things honest and good. Born without a name in the 1920s, the character was later christened as the “Little Utz Girl” by customers. It was a name that stuck, and Utz later adopted it.

What does Utz chips stand for?

A Hanover couple by the name of William and Salie are responsible for the Utz empire. The Utzes started cooking their chips (initially called Hanover Home Brand Potato Chips) in their own kitchen at a rate of about 50lbs an hour in 1921.
