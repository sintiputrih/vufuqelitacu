Who Is Allison Huppert Baby Father?

Allison Huppert Age

Allison Huppert Job

Allison is building her career in the catering business. According to LinkedIn, she started her career as executive meeting manager at Salamander Hotels & Resorts in August 2013. While working there she customized proposals, contracts, and programs to include food & beverage selections to fit clients’ unique needs and requests. Moreover, she also coordinated all details to appear on each Banquet Event Order and Group Resume. Details to coordinate include meeting room set-up specifications, banquet food & beverage items, audiovisual equipment, billing details, and special requests.

After working in that role till February 2017, she joined Lansdowne Resort and Spa as a Catering Sales Manager. She quit her job in December 2020 and joined Allstate Insurance: Michael Angles in December 2020. She continued working for the company for four months till March 2021.

Starting from January 2022, Allison has been working as Director of Catering & Events at Robert Trent Jones Golf Club in Gainesville, Virginia.

Meanwhile, on her Facebook, she shared that she worked for Salamander Resort & Spa from 2014 to 2017. Moreover, she also worked at Bungalow Alehouse – Potomac Mills.

As for education, Allison completed her high school education at Battlefield High School in 2012.

Furthermore, she has also indulged in fashion modeling. Her Instagram serves as her portfolio.
