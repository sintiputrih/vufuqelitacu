Logan Palmer Biography – Logan Palmer Wiki

Logan Palmer is a Production Technician and a reality television personality from San Diego, California. He currently serves as a Production Technician at Raindrop in San Diego. He previously worked as a Camera Operator at San Diego Padres. Palmer had earlier worked as a Videographer and Editor as well as a Production Specialist at KUSI Television in San Diego, California.

According to his LinkedIn profile, Palmer has previously worked as a Production Assistant at Petco. He has also worked as a Project Coordinator and Digital Marketing Intern at a Phoenix advertising agency. From October 2018 to March 2019, he worked as a Production Coordinator at EXOS in Phoenix, Arizona. He has also served as a Public Relations Intern at Coconino County Arizona and at AEC Printing Solutions Inc. in Flagstaff, Arizona Area. Palmer attended Northern Arizona University where he earned his Bachelor’s degree, Strategic Communications with an Emphasis in Advertising in 2018.
