WWE Fires Another Strings Of Employees From Their Corporate Offices

There were already rumors of cuts were in the air for WWE, and now they are doing just that. It appears that the company has fired more people on a Friday. These cuts are for their corporate offices, just as they moved into a beautiful, brand-new, headquarters.

WWE fired over 100 employees from their corporate offices not too long ago. In the end, the comprehensive list of people that WWE fired included executives and former World Champion.

Brandon Thurston has reported that WWE released even more employees from their corporate offices today. The number was not mentioned in the report, but this adds to the large number of people who lost their jobs with WWE over the last few months.

WWE has laid off more corporate employees today, according to multiple people at the company.

This follows layoffs of more than 100 employees in September following the closing of the TKO merger.

Sean Sapp later posted on X to confirm this report. “Unfortunate news. WWE wouldn’t confirm to me, but I’m also hearing this,” he posted as he quote-reposted the post.

McKenzie Mitchell was also released from her contract today. We will have to see if more names are made known, but this report did not mention any roster cuts.
