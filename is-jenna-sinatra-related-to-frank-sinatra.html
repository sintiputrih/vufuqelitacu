Is Jenna Sinatra related to Frank Sinatra?

Short answer – Jenna Sinatra and Frank Sinatra share a last name, but are they related? It’s a question that has been asked by many fans of the singer. Frank had three grandchildren, but Jenna keeps her family life private. The answer is not clear-cut, however, the two appear unrelated.

Who is Frank Sinatra?

@Getty Images

Frank Sinatra starred in motion pictures and was one of the most well-known singers of his time.

Family & History

On December 12, 1915, Francis Albert Sinatra was born to his parents, Anthony Martin Sinatra and Natalie Della Garaventa.

At the time, they lived in Hoboken, New Jersey. Frank had two sisters, Tina and Nancy.

Anthony Sinatra was born on May 4, 1892.

He was born in Italy, and he had two sisters and a brother.

The Sinatra family emigrated to New York City. He never learned how to read, but he did have several jobs, including firefighting, boxing, and owning a bar.

Natalina Marie Vittoria Garaventa was also born in Italy, on December 26, 1896.

She moved to the United States with her family when she was still a baby.

Her beauty earned her the nickname “Dolly” when she grew up.

When she met Anthony, her parents disapproved of their match, so the two decided to elope on Valentine’s Day, a year before Frank would be born. Natalie also enjoyed singing.

Frank Sinatra worked actively toward his future in his teen years.

He attended Hoboken High School, but he ultimately decided to drop out and focus on his singing career.

He started with small, local clubs after being inspired by Bing Crosby.

Wife & Children

While he was still a teenager, he met his first wife, Nancy Rose Barbato.

Frank Sinatra and Nancy Rose Barbato – @Getty

Nancy was born on March 25, 1917, in New Jersey.

She was married to Frank, in 1939, for over ten years.

They had three children together before they got a divorce in 1951.

Despite the divorce, Nancy remained a close friend of Frank’s for the rest of his life.

Ava Gardner was born in 1922 in North Carolina.

Frank Sinatra and Ava Gardner – @Getty

Gardner had six siblings, and their family struggled financially when she was growing up. However, Ava became an actress and was quite successful in Hollywood.

She caught Frank Sinatra’s eye in 1951, resulting in him leaving his wife Nancy to marry her.

Their marriage was very passionate, but it swayed between love and loathing until they divorced in 1957.

María de Lourdes Villiers Farrow was Frank Sinatra’s third wife.

She was born in 1945 and was 21 when she married 50-year-old Sinatra.

Mia was known for her modeling and acting profession.

Their relationship failed miserably, so she was only Sinatra’s wife for two years.

Barbara Blakeley, born in 1927, was Frank Sinatra’s final wife, and they remained married until he passed away in 1998.

Frank Sinatra and Barbara Blakeley – @Getty

Barbara, like Mia, had been a model before she met Frank.

She was also successful as an entertainer in Las Vegas.

Together, Frank and Barbara started a nonprofit that would provide therapy to children who had been abused.

Frank’s oldest daughter, Nancy Sandra Sinatra, was born in 1940.

Nancy became a famous singer herself, best known for her song “These Boots Are Made for Walkin’.”

Nancy hit the top 100 charts with over a dozen of her songs.

Francis Wayne Sinatra, or Frank Sinatra Jr., was born on January 10, 1944, in New Jersey.

He became a professional singer, songwriter, and actor like his father.

His history is fascinating because he was actually kidnapped when he was 19 years old.

The three kidnappers planned to hold him for ransom to get some of his father’s wealth.

Frank’s youngest daughter, Tina, was born in 1948 in Los Angeles, California.

Christina Sinatra immensely enjoyed acting more than singing, though she is known for both.

Christina also had some success with media production and was nominated for an Emmy Award.

Success

Frank’s success started with a local radio competition.

He was part of the Hoboken Four group for a short time, but Frank always dreamed of more for himself.

He was finally discovered by the James band when he was singing and working as a waiter.

Frank didn’t stay with one band.

Instead, he helped create ten songs with the James band, which allowed him to gain a spot with the Dorsey band.

Together, Frank and the new band produced dozens of songs before Frank decided to take the risk and go solo with his singing career.

Frank was very successful as a solo singer. However, he hit a few bumps that caused him to lapse into depression.

Multiple things caused this hardship.

For instance, his vocal cords were injured from overuse.

He also hit a point when his music declined in popularity.

Sinatra was known for being rude to journalists, and he had connections in the crime world.

He also began his acting career when he worked as a solo singer.

He appeared in several films in his time and was one of the most popular singers that also acted.

Some of his best works included The Manchurian Candidate, From Here to Eternity, and The Man with The Golden Arm.

He collaborated with his children at least once as well.

Is Jenna Sinatra related to Frank Sinatra?

Frank Sinatra and Jenna Sinatra share a last name, but are they related?

So, it’s hard to say that there is a connection without more information, but they appear unrelated.
