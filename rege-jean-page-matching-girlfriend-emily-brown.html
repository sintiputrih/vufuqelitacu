Reg-Jean Page Matched Girlfriend Emily Brown on the Red Carpet

Dear reader, it is with a heavy heart that I inform you that Your Grace has been spotted once again with his girlfriend, promenading the red carpet. And this time they donned matching velour garments — from the modiste, no doubt.

OK, all Bridgerton jokes and references aside, waking up and seeing Regé-Jean Page match his girlfriend is certainly no way to start a Monday morning, but here we are. Page and his girlfriend Emily Brown (a copywriter and soccer player) made a rare red carpet appearance together on Sunday night at the premiere of The Tragedy of Macbeth at the London Film Festival wearing matching all-black looks.

The Bridgerton breakout star was dashing in a dapper velvet tuxedo, complete with a double-breasted satin lapel jacket, white pocket square, and patent leather dress shoes.

Brown wore a matching fitted velvet A-line dress, accessorized with a deep green velvet clutch and matching platform sandals. Her blonde hair was pulled into a low, sleek pony, and Page put his hand on her back (ugh) as they walked the carpet together.

At one point before heading into the venue, Page flashed his signature smolder and eyebrow raise — you know, the same one in that spoon scene— as he took a phone call next to Brown. God, I could watch him do mundane activities all day long.

All I can say is that as a fellow petite blonde, I have managed to maintain the tiniest sliver of hope.
