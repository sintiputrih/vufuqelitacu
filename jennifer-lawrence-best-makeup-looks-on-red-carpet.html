Jennifer Lawrence shows stunning makeup looks on red carpet

Jennifer Lawrence was dubbed the 'hottest actress on the planet' by BAFTA host Joanna Lumley, and with good reason - the highly-acclaimed movie star always looks amazing, showcasing stunning outfits and gorgeous beauty looks.

In the last week alone, Jennifer, 27, has revealed four different makeup styles on the red carpet. From a bold red lip, to sultry smokey eyes and the natural look, the Hunger Games star has proved she can carry off any look going.

At the London photo call for her new movie Red Sparrow on 20 February, Jennifer wowed with glamourous, metallic eyes. The star used a shimmering blue-grey eyeshadow and strong sweep of black eyeliner above her upper lashes. She kept her lips and cheeks a neutral shade to balance the look, and wore her hair down.
