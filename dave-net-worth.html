Lifestyle

Prominent rapper and producer Dave lives a luxurious lifestyle with a net worth of $1.2 million.

Dave’s father got deported to Nigeria when he was only a few months old because he mistakenly assumed he was on a missionary visa instead of a visitor’s access.

On the other hand, his mother was permitted to stay and raise her children.

He grew up with two older brothers, Benjamin and Christopher. During Dave’s adolescence, both of his older siblings were in prison.
