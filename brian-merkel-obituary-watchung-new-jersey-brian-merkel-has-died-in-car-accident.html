The sudden departure of Ken leaves an irreplaceable void in the lives of those who loved him. His infectious laughter, unwavering support, and zest for life will be dearly missed. As we mourn his loss, let us celebrate the profound impact Ken had on our lives and hold onto the memories that will forever be etched in our hearts.
