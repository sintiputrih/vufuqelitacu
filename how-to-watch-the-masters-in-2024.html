How to watch the Masters in 2024

The Masters Tournament is one of the most prestigious events in the world of golf, attracting millions of viewers from around the globe. If you're looking to catch all the action of the 2024 Masters, here's a comprehensive guide on how to watch the tournament, including TV coverage, online streaming options, schedule, and more.

TV Coverage

CBS and ESPN

The Masters Tournament typically receives extensive TV coverage, with CBS and ESPN being the primary broadcasters. CBS traditionally covers the weekend rounds, including the highly anticipated final day, while ESPN provides coverage of the earlier rounds. Viewers can expect in-depth commentary, analysis, player interviews, and stunning visuals of the Augusta National Golf Club.

Online Streaming

Masters Official Website and App

For those who prefer to stream the tournament online, the official Masters website and app offer a seamless streaming experience. These platforms often provide multiple live streams, allowing viewers to choose specific player groups or featured holes to watch. Additionally, the website and app may offer exclusive content and behind-the-scenes coverage, enhancing the overall viewing experience.

Streaming Services

Several streaming services, such as Hulu + Live TV, YouTube TV, and Sling TV, typically offer live TV streaming packages that include both CBS and ESPN. Subscribing to these services can provide access to the Masters Tournament along with a variety of other channels, offering flexibility and convenience for viewers.

Schedule

The 2024 Masters Tournament is scheduled to take place from April 8th to April 14th, with practice rounds commencing earlier in the week. The tournament's schedule usually includes practice rounds, the Par 3 Contest, and the four main rounds of competitive play, culminating in the crowning of the coveted Green Jacket to the champion.

International Viewers

International Broadcasters

For international viewers, various broadcasters around the world secure rights to televise the Masters Tournament. These broadcasters often provide their own schedules and coverage details, catering to the local audience. Checking with local sports networks or the official Masters website can help international fans find the best ways to watch the tournament from their respective regions.

Mobile Viewing

Mobile Apps and Services

In today's digital age, watching the Masters on mobile devices has become increasingly popular. With the official Masters app, viewers can stay updated with live scores, player statistics, and video highlights while on the go. Additionally, streaming services and TV network apps often offer mobile viewing options, ensuring that fans can enjoy the tournament from virtually anywhere.

The Masters Tournament in 2024 promises to deliver thrilling golf action and captivating moments on the historic grounds of Augusta National. Whether you prefer traditional TV viewing, online streaming, or mobile access, there are various ways to ensure that you don't miss a single stroke of this prestigious event. By leveraging the available TV coverage, online streaming platforms, and mobile apps, golf enthusiasts can immerse themselves in the excitement of the Masters and witness the crowning of the next champion.
