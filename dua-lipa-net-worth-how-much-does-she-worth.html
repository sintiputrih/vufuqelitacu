Dua Lipa net worth : How much does she worth?

Dua Lipa is a British- Albanian singer and songwriter who  is known for her signature disco-pop sound. In this Article, we will be taking a great look into Dua Lipa Net worth in a quest to know rich she is.

Who is Dua Lipa? 

Dua Lipa was born on 22nd August 1995 London, England as the eldest child of her Kosovo Albanian parents Anes, her mother  and Dukagjin Lipa, her father. 

On 27 November 2022, Lipa was granted Albanian citizenship by president Bajram Begaj, one day before the country’s 110th independence celebrations.

Dua Lipa grew up in West Hampstead, London She attended Fitzjohn’s Primary School, and with a high ambition of becoming a musician, she took music lessons including the cello.

She however failed to enter the school choir When she auditioned and that actually came as a motivation for her to take her singing lessons even more seriously. 

At the age of nine, she began weekend singing lessons at the Sylvia Young Theatre School where she dedicated so much efforts into working on her voice and singing abilities.
