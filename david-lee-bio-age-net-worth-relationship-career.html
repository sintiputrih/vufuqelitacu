How did David Lee start his Professional Career?

In 2003, David Lee joined Long Beach State’s junior squad. His professional volleyball playing career took him to Greece, Russia, Italy, France, Puerto Rico, Portugal, and Indonesia, among other places.

He also won Greek and Indonesian national championships. In 2005, Lee made his debut for the US national team. Lee won a gold medal with his squad in the 2008 Olympics.

Along with competing in a variety of leagues and competitions, he won gold in the 2005 America’s Cup, bronze in the 2007 FIVB World League, and silver in the 2009 NORCECA Championship.
