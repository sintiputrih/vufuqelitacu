Cardi B Shuts Down Pregnancy Rumors With Graphic Tampon Suggestion

Earlier this month, Cardi B revealed her height and weight to her millions of followers online. The move was surprising, only because there is such a stigma around women and their weight in our society. Since then, she's started putting on some of the pounds that she lost while nursing her baby Kulture. She complained that she just kept losing weight, wanting her curvy figure back and now that she's working her way back to it, some fans are convinced that she's pregnant with her second child. Well, in true Cardi B fashion, the superstar told everyone that she's not expecting a baby and she offered to prove it.

"BARDIGANG is harrassing me cause I’m gaining weight again," she said on Twitter. That's where things took a turn with the rapper suggesting that she might need to show everybody hard proof that she's not pregnant. "I might have to pull a tampon out my pussy so ya can stop. Like damn my baby ain’t even crawling yet can ya stop with the assumptions !"
