Bri Bagwell Country Musician

Bagwell began having a passion for music when she was still in high school. She would compose and sing songs with her siblings. When she completed high school, Bri moved to Austin Texas to join the University of Texas to study marketing. During her free time, she would perform at local clubs which made her become familiar with most of the residents in that area.

After she graduated from university, she put her whole focus on music. She became a contestant at a talent competition for country artists on Next Superstar on CMT. Unfortunately, she was eliminated during the fourth week. Her participation in the show brought her exposure as people came to know about her and her music.

Bri went ahead and released her first self titled album and a seven-song EP in 2013. Bagwell released her second album When a Heart Breaks which received positive reviews. The song My Boots became a hit song around Texas.
