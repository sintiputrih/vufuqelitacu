Everything We Know About Giada Di Laurentiis's Boyfriend

There are some Food Network stars who people just instantly connect with and it's really easy to love Giada DiLaurentiis. The culinary talent combines a beachy, California style with the Italian food that she has grown up with, and she has become super successful as a result. Giada De Laurentiis has a $20 million net worth and fans have loved watching her cook her easy yet delicious meals on many different TV shows. Giada De Laurentiis got divorced from her husband Todd Thompson several years ago and fans have been curious about Giada's new relationship as it seems like things have been going great.

While it's definitely sad that Giada got divorced, she has found love again with Shane Farley. The two have been together since 2015, according to The Sun. Keep reading to find out everything that we know about Giada De Laurentii's boyfriend, Shane Farley, and how Giada and Shane's relationship has been going.

8 Shane Gets Along Well With Giada's Daughter Jade

Fans of Giada's have seen many adorable photos on her Instagram account of her daughter Jade, who has been growing up before everyone's eyes. Fans might be curious about how Shane and Jade get along and it sounds like things have been great between them.

According to People, Shane explained that he enjoys hanging out with Jade as he thinks so highly of her. Shane said,

“I do not have children and to be able to have such a well-adjusted, fun kid around has been really nice. She’s just a great child. She’s smart. She’s funny. She’s adorable.”

7 Shane Produced 'The Rachel Ray Show'

If people are curious about what Giada's boyfriend does for a living, Shane Farley is a TV producer.

According to The Sun, Shane produced The Rachel Ray Show and was nominated for five Emmy Awards, two of which he won. Shane held this producing job for six years. The Rachel Ray Show began airing in 2006 and there have been 16 seasons so far. Some of Shane's other producing credits include Steve Harvey's show Steve, The Gossip Table, Big Evening Buzz with Carrie Keegan, and FabLife.

6 Giada Said Spending Time Together During The COVID-19 Pandemic Helped Them

When talking about being in quarantine with Shane Farley during the COVID-19 pandemic, Giada said that this was a positive thing for their relationship. Many celebrity couples have shared their experiences with staying home together and being around each other much more than usual.

5 The Couple Met Because They Worked On A Show Together

Fans who want to know how Giada and Shane met will be happy to hear that Shane was working as a producer for a TV episode that Bobby Flay and Giada appeared on.

Cheat Sheet reported that Giada and Shane waited to start their relationship, as Giada got divorced and then she explained that she was single for a while.

4 Shane Was Married To Jennifer Giamo

As for Shane's romantic past before he began dating Giada, he was married to Jennifer Giamo. According to Us Weekly, Jennifer is a personal trainer and Shane decided to file for divorce in March 2015.

As a source explained to Page Six about the situation, "Jen’s been reaching out to all Shane’s acquaintances through Facebook. She’s trying to see if Shane started dating Giada while she was still with him.”

3 The Couple Went To Hawaii With Giada's Daughter Jade

Shane Farley and Giada Di Laurentiis went on a family vacation to Hawaii. According to People, in June 2021, Giada, Shane, and Giada's 13-year-old daughter Jade traveled to Maui.

Giada talked about the trip on her Instagram account, writing that she and Jade went sailing and snorkeling. The three looked so happy in all of the photos.

Before he became a TV producer, Shane was always a big fan of TV shows. Mashed.com reported that he loved watching late night talk shows and he realized that he could be a producer when he watched David Letterman talk to Robert Morton, who worked as his executive producer, during one episode.
