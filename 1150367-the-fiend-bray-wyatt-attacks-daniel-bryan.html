The Fiend Bray Wyatt Takes Out Daniel Bryan Backstage At SmackDown

Backstage at WWE SmackDown, Sami Zayn was trying to convince Daniel Bryan to join him and Shinsuke Nakamura. However, the room went dark and “The Fiend” Bray Wyatt appeared behind Bryan. He locked in the Mandible Claw and choked out Bryan.
