Jack White Hall (born 7 July 1988) is a British comedian, actor, writer and television presenter from London, England. He gained popularity as a stand-up comedian. Also, the 30-year-old comedian performed his first stand-up comedy show at the “Edinburgh Fringe Festival.”

The star was later nominated for the “Best Newcomer” award at the 2008 Chortle Awards. His real full birth name is Jack Peter Benedict Whitehall.

Jack was also a regular on “Stand Up for the Week.” As an actor, he made his big screen debut in the comedy film “The Bad Education Movie” as Alfie Wickers.

The comedian has also won the “King of Comedy” award three times at the British Comedy Awards. The actor appeared in movies like Mother’s Day (2016) and The Nutcracker and the Four Realms (2018). In addition, Whitehall also provided the voice for him in the movie Asterix: The Mansions of the Gods.

The various TV series that Jack appeared in are Noah’s Ark, Little Crackers, Fresh Meat (2011-2016), Bad Education, Backchat, Drunk History, etc. The comedian is currently working on “Thunderbirds Are Go” (2015 to present).

Early years, parents, childhood and profile

The actor was born on July 7, 1988 in the city of Westminster, London, England. Therefore, the age of Jack Whitehall is 30 years old, as of 2018. His mother “Hilary Amanda Jane Whitehall” was also an actress.

Also, Jack’s father, “Michael John Whitehall,” was a television producer. He worked as an agent for famous actors like Judi Dench, Barry Chuckle, and Richard Griffiths. The actor also has a younger sister, Molly Louisa, and a younger brother, Barnaby William.

Additionally, he enrolled at Tower House School and later attended Dragon School in Oxford. Later, he joined Marlborough College. The famous comedian attended the University of Manchester to study art history.

At the age of 9, Jack appeared in the series «Noah’s Ark». His passion for stand-up comedy grew in his college days. Thus, he came in second in the “Laughing Horse New Act of the Year” competition.

Personal affairs, girlfriend and spouse

The 30-year-old comedian was in a relationship with his girlfriend Gemma Chan (British actress). They started dating in 2011. The couple met on the sets of Fresh Meat.
