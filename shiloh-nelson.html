Wiki/Biography

Shiloh Nelson was born on Friday, January 23, 2009 (age 11 years; as in 2020), in Portland, Oregon. Her zodiac sign is Aquarius. Now, she lives in Los Angeles with her family.

Physical Appearance

Eye Color: Green

Hair Color: Brown

Family & Ethnicity

Shiloh Nelson is the daughter of the film producer and actor John Phillip Nelson.

Shiloh Nelson with her Mother

She has seven brothers, Elijah Nelson (actor), Josiah Nelson (actor), Shawn Nelson (film director), David Nelson (actor), Micah Nelson (actor), Daniel Nelson (actor), and Judah Nelson (actor); and a sister, Mary Nelson (actress).

Career

She made her acting debut with the film A Walk in My Shoes (2010) as a ‘rescued child.’

A Walk in My Shoes (2010)

She was cast as a toddler in the films, Adopting Terror (2012), and Thomas & the Trainmaster (2013). Her prominent roles came with the films, Mom’s Night Out (2014), and Annabelle (2014).

Shiloh Nelson in Mom’s Night Out (2014)

She went to act in the films, Bambi Cottages (2014), Tomorrowland (2015), A Fairy’s Game (2018), and Secret Agent Dingledorf and His Trusty Dog Splat (2020). She made her television debut with the series ‘General Hospital’ (2012) as ‘Hope.’

Shiloh Nelson in General Hospital (2012)

She has acted in the famous TV series, Two and a Half Men (2014), Grandfathered (2016), How to Get Away with Murder (2016), Criminal Minds (2018), NCIS: Los Angeles (2019), and Puppy Dog Pals (2018-19).

Facts/Trivia

  • She has a TikTok channel with her brothers called ‘Shiloh & Bros’ in which she posts videos with her brothers, mainly Elijah. They got famous after taking on the Jimmy Fallon hashtag challenge for The Tonight Show. She has more than a million followers on her TikTok account.
  • She also has a YouTube channel, ‘Shiloh and Bros.’ She has more than 240K subscribers on her channel.
  • In 2020, she starred in Netflix’s film Feel the Beat (2020) as ‘Ruby.’
