Ronald Lauder Biography

Ronald Lauder is an American Businessman, Billionaire, Philanthropist, Art Collector, and Political Activist, who was born and brought up New York City, New York, United States of America. He is widely known as the President of the ‘World Jewish Congress’ since 2007, and as the sole heirs to the ‘Estée Lauder cosmetics company’ with his brother Leonard Lauder, which was founded by their parents.

Ronald opened the Neue Galerie in New York, an art museum across the street from the Metropolitan Museum of Art on November 16, 2001. He has been a great supporter of Donald Trump and made over $1.6 million to pro-Donald Trump organizations in 2016. He founded ‘Lauder Business School’ in Vienna, Austria and became its president in 2003.

    The Ronald Lauder Foundation

    Back in 1987, Lauder established the Ronald S. Lauder Foundation, a philanthropic organization that is dedicated to rebuilding Jewish life in Central including Eastern Europe. The foundation also supports student exchange programs among New York including various capitals in Central and Eastern Europe.

    Ronald Lauder Woman In Gold

    Woman in Gold is a 2015 biographical drama film which was directed by Simon Curtis. It was written by Alexi Kaye Campbell. It stars Helen Mirren, Ryan Reynolds, Daniel Brühl, Katie Holmes, Tatiana Maslany, Max Irons, Charles Dance, Elizabeth McGovern, including Jonathan Pryce.

    The drama film is based on the true story of Maria Altmann, an elderly Jewish refugee living in Cheviot Hills, Los Angeles. Maria, who, together with her young lawyer, Randy Schoenberg, fought the government of Austria for almost a decade reclaiming Gustav Klimt’s iconic painting of her aunt, Portrait of Adele Bloch-Bauer I, which was stolen from Maria’s relatives by the Nazis in Vienna just before the World War II.

    Altmann then took her legal battle all the way to the Supreme Court of the United States, which ruled on the case Republic of Austria v. Altmann in 2004. The film was screened in the Berlinale Special Galas section of the 65th Berlin International Film Festival earlier on 9 February 2015, and was then released in the United Kingdom on 1 April 2015 and in the United States on 10 April the same year. Ben Miles plays the role of Ronald Lauder.

    Ronald Lauder Alzheimer’s Research

    Back in 1998, Lauder co-founded the Alzheimer’s Drug Discovery Foundation together with his brother, Leonard A. Lauder. Its mission is to “rapidly accelerate the discovery of drugs to prevent, treat and also cure Alzheimer’s disease.” Ronald continues as the Foundation’s co-chairman.

    Ronald Lauder Accusations by Austrian Jewish community leader

    The Israelitische Kultusgemeinde Wien (Vienna Israelite Community) stopped Lauder for allegedly interfering in its board elections. IKG President Oskar Deutsch also alleged that Lauder had offered incentives to members of the IKG to support another candidate. A Lauder spokesman opposed the allegations. In January 2013, it was reported that Deutsch together with Lauder had resolved their dispute.
