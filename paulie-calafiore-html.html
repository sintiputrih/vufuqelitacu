Paulie Calafiore - Age, Family, Bio

DJ turned reality star who entered the house of Big Brother for the show's 18th season on CBS. He'd later compete on MTV's The Challenge: Final Reckoning. He has opened up a gym in his hometown to develop athletes.

Before Fame

He was a three-time captain of his high school soccer team before heading to Rutgers University, where he was a star for the Scarlet Knights soccer team.

Trivia

He enjoys Krav Maga among other martial arts as well as playing the guitar.

Family Life

His younger brother Cody was a house guest on the 16th season of Big Brother and finished as the runner-up. He has a younger sister as well. He briefly dated Bachelor in Paradise's Danielle Maltby in 2018 and subsequently began dating Cara Maria Sorbello from MTV's The Challenge.

Associated With

He competed against Da'Vonne Rogers on the 18th season of Big Brother.
