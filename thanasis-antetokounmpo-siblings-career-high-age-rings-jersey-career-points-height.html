Thanasis Antetokounmpo Siblings, Career High, Age, Rings, Jersey, Career Points, Height

Thanasis Antetokounmpo is a Greek-Nigerian professional basketball player for the Milwaukee Bucks of the National Basketball Association.

He plays the small forward and power forward positions.

Antetokounmpo commenced his professional basketball career with Filathlitikos, of the Greek third division in 2012. He was then selected by the Delaware 87ers with the ninth overall pick in the 2013 NBA Development League Draft on November 1, 2013.

Antetokounmpo was subsequently selected with the 51st overall pick in the 2014 NBA draft by the New York Knicks on June 26, 2014.

Prior to joining the Milwaukee Bucks in 2019, Antetokounmpo had a stint with Andorra (2016–2017) and Panathinaikos (2017–2019).

Thanasis Antetokounmpo Siblings

Thanasis Antetokounmpo is the older brother of two-time NBA MVP Giannis Antetokounmpo as well as Kostas Antetokounmpo and Alex Antetokounmpo.

Thanasis Antetokounmpo Career High
