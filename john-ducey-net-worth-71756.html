John Ducey Net Worth

He was a series regular on Oh, Grow Up (Alan Ball's ABC sitcom prior to Six Feet Under), playing the role of Ford, and had recurring roles as Leonard on the WB's Sabrina, the Teenage Witch (in addition to a guest appearance several years earlier), and Jack's station manager Jamie on Will & Grace. Ducey made a brief appearance on Malcolm in the Middle, in episode No. 119, as a Lawyer giving Hal the will to the Wilkersons' neighbors. Since 2003, he has appeared on According to Jim, Scrubs, Joey, Freddie, Hot Properties, My Name Is Earl, Desperate Housewives, How I Met Your Mother, Rules of Engagement and Hot in Cleveland. He played Tom Lucas, the brothers' dad, on the Disney show Jonas in season one. He has worked on Drake & Josh as the husband of a couple who has stayed in the Drake and Josh Inn. He also played Dr. Donald Spaulding in The Suite Life Movie.
